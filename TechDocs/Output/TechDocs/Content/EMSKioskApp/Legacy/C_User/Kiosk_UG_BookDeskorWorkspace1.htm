Book a Desk or Workspace in EMS Kiosk (Legacy)

You use the Make a Reservation page in EMS Kiosk to make your own reservations for today.

Note: The following procedure is written from the perspective of a group search; however, depending on how your EMS Kiosk is configured, you might be required to enter a personnel number/employee ID number for your search criteria.

  1. Click Make a Reservation. A page opens where you find your user profile in the system (unless your system is set up to allow guest reservations).

Group/Person Search Page

  1. In the Group field (which may be labeled anything your Administrator prefers, such as "Employee"), enter your name.
  2. Click Search. A list of all groups/people that meet your search criteria is displayed. The search is limited to the exact order of the characters in the string, but the string can be found anywhere in the search results, and it is not case-sensitive. For example, a search string of “ed” returns both Anderson, Scott and Evans, Dean.

Sample Results for a Group/Person

  1. Click the icon next to your name in the search results. 
  2. The Make a Reservation page opens. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk (*).

Note: Based on how your organization has configured EMS Kiosk, various fields, including the Check-in time, Check-out time, Check-in date, and Check-out date, may or may not be available.

Make a Reservation page

  1. Modify the values for Check-in time, Check-out time, Check-in date, and/or Check-out date as needed.
  2. Specify your filtering options, and then continue to reserve space from a Reserve Space From a List or from a Reserve Space From a Map.

Reserve Space From a List

  1. Click List to display the list of available spaces in a list.

List View

  1. Click the Reserve icon next to the space that you want to reserve for your event. The Make a Reservation page closes and the My Reservations page opens. The newly scheduled event is now displayed on this page.

Reserve Space From a Map

  1. Click Map to view the available space (typically marked with a green icon) on a map.

Map view of available spaces

  1. Click the icon for the available space that you want to reserve for your event. A Space Details popup opens with an option for reserving the space.

Room Details popup

  1. Click Reserve. The selected space is reserved for your event. The Make a Reservation page closes and the My Reservations page opens. The newly scheduled event is now displayed on this page.
