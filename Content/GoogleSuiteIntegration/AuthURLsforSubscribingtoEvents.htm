Authorize URLs for Subscribing to Events

To authorize URLs for subscribing to events from GSuite:

  1. Navigate to this link.
  2. Ensure you have the correct project selected.

  1. Enter your EMS Cloud Services Domain (for example: my-subdomain.emscloudservice.com).
  2. Click Add Domain.

  1. The Verify Ownership dialog box will appear. Click Take Me There.
