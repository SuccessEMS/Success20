EMS Platform Services

EMS Platform Services is the new add-on, middle-tier component that provides a modern, scalable way for partners and customers to integrate with the EMS Platform. Platform Services enables the development of multi-platform applications that can be customized, cloud-based, scalable, and easily integrated.
