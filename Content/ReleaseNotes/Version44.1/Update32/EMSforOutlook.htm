Area

Cancellations

 

Canceling Series Occurrence Did Not Cancel in EMS

For recurring meetings created in EMS for Outlook who try to cancel one booking in the recurrence experienced the booking remaining scheduled in EMS.

Internal Reference # 40122
(For EMS Internal Use Only)

Book a Room

 

In Positive Timezones, EMS for Outlook Was Not Reading Special Dates Correctly

When booking a room in EMS for Outlook in a positive timezone, rooms were not available even if time was inside of building hours.

Fix: Modified functionality so that users can book rooms within open building hours.

Internal Reference # 42098
(For EMS Internal Use Only)

Conflict Resolution

 

Conflict Resolution Was Not Updating Room Resource Calendar

Resolved room conflicts were not reflecting on the room resource calendar.

Fix: Modified functionality so that conflict resolutions are reflected on the room resource calendar.

Internal Reference # 42241
(For EMS Internal Use Only)
