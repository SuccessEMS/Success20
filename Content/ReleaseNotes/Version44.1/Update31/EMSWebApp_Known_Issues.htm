Not all bookings showing on schedule view grid when completing a room search

Not all bookings are displaying on the schedule grid when searching for a room due to a logic error where the value in parameter ‘Number of Available Rooms Upon Search’ is being used to limit the results returned on booking searches.

For customers who are affected and require a workaround, submit an EMS Support Help Desk ticket through the EMS Customer Support Portal to receive additional information.
