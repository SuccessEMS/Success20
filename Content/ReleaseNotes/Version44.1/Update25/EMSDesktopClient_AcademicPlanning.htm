Campus Planning Interface (CPI)

Course Preferences 'Requires Different Room' Checkbox not being honored

Customer has reported that the "Requires Different" room for cross-listed courses does not seem to be behaving properly.

I got on a call with the customer and recorded what they were reporting. Prior to publishing, customer went through cross-listed courses (that exist on different domains than the parent), and marked them as "Require Different Room."

When she changes a room on the parent, whether in Banner or EMS directly, EMS then writes the new location back to all child courses, even though the checkbox is still marked on the child courses.

This causes the customer a lot of work to clean up this mess every time a room changes. (Video Attached
