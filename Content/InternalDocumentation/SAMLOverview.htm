SAML 2.0

(EMS Internal Documentation)

SAML, Security Assertion Markup Language, is the link between the authentication of a user’s identity and the authorization to use a service, such as EMS Web App or EMS Mobile App. When troubleshooting SAML issues, it is helpful to understand the SAML authentication process, in order to pinpoint where the problem is occurring.
