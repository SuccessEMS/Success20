SAML 2.0

(EMS Internal Documentation)

SAML, Security Assertion Markup Language, is an open standard for exchanging authentication and authorization data between an identity provider and a service provider. When troubleshooting SAML issues, it is important to understand the SAML authentication process in order to narrow down where in the process the problem is occurring.
