Notifications Exclude Building Closures On Notifications When set to “No,” building closures are not excluded from notification date calculations. Yes/No Parameter is set to YES.   A Building is closed on the 9th, 10th and 11th (3 days).  Today is the 4th.     A notification is set to trigger within 10 days.     Something is done on the 16th  to make notificaiton trigger.    IT WILL be triggered because even though the 16th is more than 10 days away from the 4th, we are subtracting (excluding) 3 days.    Next, something is done on the 18th to make notificaiton trigger.   It will NOT trigger because the 18th is 14 days from the 4th and when you subtract 3 closed days - you are outside of 10 days.        IF BOTH EXCLUDE BUILDING CLOSURES ON NOTIFICATIONS AND EXCLUDE WEEKENDS ON NOTIFICATIONS ARE SET TO YES, ADD NUMBER OF DAYS EXCLUDED TOGETHER IN CALCULATION. BOTH WILL BE TAKEN INTO ACCOUNT.  
Notifications Exclude Weekends On Notifications When set to “No,” weekends are not excluded from notification date calculations. Yes/No Parameter is set to Yes.   Group notification is set to remind Group 10 days before booking.   Today is the Wednesday the 4th.   A meeting is on the Wednesday the 18th - a remdinder notification will get triggered to the recipient on Wednesday the 4th because that is 14 days less 4 weekend days...(thus 10 days)     NOW:   if Group reminder is set to be sent at 4pm and the bookings on the 18th end before 4pm, no Group reminders will get triggered. (sent)   In other words - no reminders will go to recipients who have bookings that end before notification is to be sent.    IF BOTH EXCLUDE BUILDING CLOSURES ON NOTIFICATIONS AND EXCLUDE WEEKENDS ON NOTIFICATIONS ARE SET TO YES, ADD NUMBER OF DAYS EXCLUDED TOGETHER IN CALCULATION. BOTH WILL BE TAKEN INTO ACCOUNT.
